HEPA Filter

High-Efficiency Particulate Air (HEPA) filters, often referred to as absolute filters, represent the pinnacle of air purification technology. They are the ultimate solution for removing sub-micron airborne particles and are extensively used in EU-13 applications. HEPA filters guarantee an impressive 99.97% efficiency in capturing particles as small as 0.3 microns. Depending on specific needs, varying efficiencies of 50%, 65%, and 95% are also available. These filters are integral to clean rooms and are indispensable in pharmaceutical applications, ensuring exceptionally clean and contaminant-free air.
